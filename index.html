Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about setting up operations with MetroMaid.

How does valet trash increase our property Net Operating Income (NOI)?

We bill you a flat rate per door, allowing you to bundle this service as a standard premium amenity for residents. The spread between the cost and the resident structural amenity fee becomes continuous revenue directly increasing your property value.

What days and times do collection teams operate?

Our standard schedules run 5 nights a week (typically Sunday through Thursday). Residents put their provided branded totes out between 6:00 PM and 8:00 PM, and our crews finish collection by early morning.

How do you manage complex layouts or deep walk-up complexes?

Our operations leaders map out custom routes for every community before launch. We use dynamic software checking to verify that every single doorstep is cleared and documented daily.
